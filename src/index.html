Hi! Welcome to your Euchre Coach.

This Euchre Coach was designed to be a "perfect memory" for a player of the midwestern card game Euchre. I made it to help me better understand the game and its strategies, because it was hard for me to fully understand certain strategies without seeing them played out.

You can use it to visualize and play through strategies for practice, or for (perfectly legal!) card-counting during a game on another device.

Enjoy.
